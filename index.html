LHS CAD Club
We at the Leigh High School CAD Club strive to teach students who are interested in engineering, CAD, 3D Printing, and Computer Designing. We will work with softwares such as Autodesk® Inventor®, Autodesk® Fusion 360®, and AutoCAD®.
